THE JOURNEY

Walking the Talk is the world's largest culture-only consulting firm, later acquired by ZRG Partners. Their clients include Visa, Vodafone, Telstra, Westpac, Tesco Bank, eBay, Experian, Syngenta, Takeda, and Lilly. I was brought in to redesign their three core digital tools — the products that their entire consulting practice runs on.

Taylor Assessment is the only psychometric tool in the world that measures individual culture contribution across 6 archetypes and 30 behaviors. I redesigned it from the ground up in partnership with Psynet, the psychometrics engine behind it. The new Taylor Assessment was press-released globally in January 2024. It's now used by over 50,000 leaders in Walking the Talk's database.

Culture Insights Survey measures 90 behaviors across multiple languages. It's a 15-20 minute assessment that gives organizations immediate portal results — no waiting weeks for consultants to compile data. I redesigned the survey experience and the results portal so that HR leaders and executives could self-serve insights without needing a consultant on the call.

Target Culture Mapping is the workshop tool — used in 80+ executive workshops to help leadership teams align on the culture they want to build. I redesigned the digital experience that supports these in-person sessions, making it easier for facilitators to capture, visualize, and share outcomes in real time.

The common thread across all three tools: taking complex psychometric and organizational data and making it immediately useful. No dashboards for the sake of dashboards. Every screen had to answer a specific question that a CEO or CHRO would actually ask.

THE WORK

Bolt VPN had 25 million+ downloads but a dated interface that was losing users to slicker competitors. I was brought in through Galixo AI to do a complete UI/UX transformation — rethinking the core connection experience, server selection, and the paywall that drives their subscription business.

The redesign focused on clarity and speed: one-tap connect, visual server status, and a paywall flow that reduced friction while improving conversion. Clean, modern, and built for the kind of user who just wants their VPN to work without thinking about it.

THE WORK

ADIB is the Middle East's leading Islamic bank — 1.5 million customers, 80% digital adoption, Euromoney's Best Islamic Bank 2025. I joined as Lead Product Designer to work on their most strategic digital initiatives.

Remit! was the flagship project — real-time cross-border transfers powered by Visa Direct, reaching 11 billion+ cards globally. I designed the end-to-end experience: recipient management, transfer flow, fee transparency, confirmation states, and error handling for a product that had to work across dozens of corridors with different regulatory requirements.

I also designed the open banking UI/UX for the UAE's emerging Open Finance ecosystem — account aggregation, consent management, and third-party data sharing flows that had to balance regulatory compliance with genuine usability. And I led a design system overhaul, taking a chaotic collection of inconsistent components and restructuring it into an atomic system that the team could actually maintain.

The tenure was short — six months — and the environment was slow and political. But the impact was recognized. I shipped meaningful work in a fraction of the time most enterprise designers get to warm up.

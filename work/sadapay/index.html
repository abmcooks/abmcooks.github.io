SadaPay

Website LinkedIn Instagram

1 designer. 12 engineers. 220 million people — 87% of whom had never used a bank. I was the first in-house designer at SadaPay. The app I designed in year one helped secure a $7.2M seed round — the largest by a Pakistani startup at the time.

Over 3.5 years, I built everything: the core banking app, SadaDS (a design system with tokens connected to production code), the MEA region’s first numberless debit card, SadaBiz (a freelancer payment platform with Apple Pay and Google Pay), and the fraud service blueprint that the CEO calls my “superpower.” I scaled the design team from 1 to 8 designers, built the career framework, and created a culture where the newest junior could challenge the lead’s work.

The company grew from seed to $50M acquisition by Papara. The 4.8-star rating and $1.5B in annual volume came from a design foundation built to earn trust in a market where trust didn’t exist.

My first professional design role. Walking the Talk is a culture transformation consultancy — part of ZRG Partners — working with 200+ organizations across 40+ countries to shift how companies operate and think.

I designed digital tools and visual systems for culture change programs across global enterprises. This is where I learned the fundamentals: working within constraints, designing for diverse audiences, and understanding that design isn’t just screens — it’s how systems shape behavior. The thread that runs from here to fintech started with one question: how do you design trust?

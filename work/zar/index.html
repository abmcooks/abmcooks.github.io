I joined ZAR as the founding designer — the first design hire for an a16z-backed Solana wallet building digital dollar infrastructure for emerging markets. No design system, no established flows, no research. Just a React Native app and a thesis that stablecoins could replace cash.

I built the design system from zero (670+ components), then flew to Pakistan to watch how merchants actually use the product. That research revealed 72% of transactions were happening through Cash QR — merchants had completely bypassed our order system. I redesigned the core experience around their behavior, not against it. Cut onboarding from 18 steps to 4. Established UX governance, brand compliance, and scaled the design team.

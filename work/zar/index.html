ZAR

Website LinkedIn Instagram

I joined ZAR as the founding designer — the first design hire for an a16z-backed Solana wallet building digital dollar infrastructure for emerging markets. No design system, no established flows, no research. Just a thesis that stablecoins could replace cash.

I built the design system from zero (670+ components) and led on-ground user research in Pakistan — interviewing freelancers, remittance senders, shopkeepers — to understand how people actually use dollars in their daily lives. That research shaped every design decision. Established UX governance, brand compliance, and scaled the design team.

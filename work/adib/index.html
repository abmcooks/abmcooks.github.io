ADIB’s design system was chaotic — inconsistent components, no atomic structure, engineers interpreting specs differently across teams. I came in to fix it. Rebuilt the system from chaotic to atomic, established design governance, and created documentation that engineering teams actually followed.

Alongside the system rescue, I shipped two major products: the Open Banking experience (built from zero with Lean Technologies) and Remit! — a Visa Direct remittance product that made ADIB the first bank worldwide to offer 11+ billion payment endpoints. Enterprise banking, regulatory constraints, stakeholder complexity — a different kind of design challenge from startup speed.
